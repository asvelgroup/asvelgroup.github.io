Our Identity

Built in Dubai.
Operating the World.

ASVEL Group is a Dubai-based diversified conglomerate built on the belief that a truly diversified business — rooted in integrity and guided by ambition — can create lasting impact across every sector it enters.

From gold trading and real estate to IT solutions, event management, sports, and a portfolio of investment opportunities spanning food distribution, retail, hospitality, and wholesale trading — we have built a group that reflects the breadth and dynamism of modern commerce.

We are operators, investors, advisors, and innovators. Ten distinct sectors. One unified vision.

We do not follow markets. We build the ecosystems that markets depend on.
